Here are my 5GO TOrecipes.

  1. Pizza

    2. Unknown to many this is one of the quickest meals one can whip up on a lazy Tuesday Night.

    All you need to make is some good Marinara Sauce,grilled chicken and instant yeast to make that pizza dough in 30 tops.

  2. One pot oven baked Roast

    3. Potatoes,broccoli ,carrots and some grilled chicken-Peel some potatoes,shape em, toss tem in the oven

  3. Ugali and boiled meat

    4. Boil the meat of choice as you do other chores when softened make the ugali

  4. Pilau

    5. With left over meat in the fridge, fry onions, dry cinnamon,candamom,cumin,ginger and garlic

    When the meat has absorbed the added in spices, toss in the rice then add water

    As you fold laundry, the pilau will be ready in no time
